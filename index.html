About Us

Spotify is a leading music streaming service that brings music to life. Our mission is to connect people to their favorite music, artists, and playlists.

Spotify was founded in 2006 and has since become one of the world's leading music streaming platforms. We've connected millions of music lovers with their favorite songs and artists.
Spotify offers a personalized music experience, from discovering new tracks to creating your playlists. We provide a wide range of songs, podcasts, and more for your enjoyment.
We have a dedicated team of music enthusiasts, engineers, and artists who work together to deliver the best music experience to our users.
Our vision is to make music accessible to everyone, anytime, anywhere. We strive to provide the best music streaming service globally.
